About us

Sethembe Funeral Services was founded in 2019
by two members, Mlibo Maqokolo & Ondela B.

Operations, and Brand Development.

Sethembe Funeral Services aims to
fill the gap in the market by offering
personalized, affordable, reliable,
and easy to access funeral cover
plans to the general public in South
Africa.
